ABOUT RD College Turf

RD College Turf offers premium artificial turf designed for professional and recreational players. With high-quality surfaces, night floodlights, safe parking and clean amenities, we ensure the best sporting experience every time. Our facility is equipped with professional-grade equipment and maintained by experienced sports professionals.

Professional Quality

FIFA-approved artificial turf with professional-grade equipment and maintenance.

Floodlit Facility

High-intensity LED floodlights for night games and extended playing hours.

Safe & Secure

24/7 security, CCTV surveillance, and ample parking for complete safety.
