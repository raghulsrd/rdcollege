CULTURAL & EVENTS

RD National College, Erode – Hackathon 2026 Event Plan

The hackathon scheduled for 27th March 2026 at RD National College, Erode, is a unique car-based competitive innovation event, combining speed, engineering, and strategic combat.

⸻

Event Timeline & Structure

• The event will commence at 3:13 PM with the formal inauguration, including felicitation of the Chief Guest and opening proceedings.

• From 4:00 PM to 4:17 PM, all eight participating houses will present their designs.

Each house will showcase:

• One racing car

• One combat (fighting) car

Participants will explain features and place their cars on designated tracks.

⸻

Main Hackathon Phase (4:17 PM – 4:47 PM)

During this 30-minute competitive phase, two parallel events will take place:

1. Racing Segment

• One car from each house will compete on a 200-meter synthetic track.

• Cars will complete maximum possible laps within 30 minutes.

• Performance will be evaluated based on total laps and leading position.

2. Combat Segment

• Simultaneously, combat cars will operate in a separate arena located ~5 meters away.

• These cars will engage in offensive and defensive interactions, using engineered mechanisms such as:

• Cutting tools

• Piercing/welding attachments

• Projectile or impact-based systems

• The objective is to disable opponents while maintaining functionality.

⸻

Evaluation & Winning Criteria

• At the end of the 30-minute phase, only houses whose combat cars remain functional will qualify.

• Among these qualified houses:

• Rankings will be determined based on racing performance (lap count and position).

• Even if a house secures top racing positions, it must have a surviving combat car to be eligible.

• Final awards:

• 1st, 2nd, and 3rd places will be awarded based on combined survival (combat) + performance (racing) criteria.

⸻

Conclusion & Valedictory

• The event will conclude by 4:57 PM, followed by:

• Prize distribution ceremony

• Closing celebrations

⸻

This hackathon is designed to encourage innovation, engineering excellence, teamwork, and strategic thinking through a highly engaging and competitive format.
